Baseball

Baseball is one of the most unique sports. There are many things about baseball that make it unique. It’s the only one of America’s big four sports (baseball, basketball, football, & hockey) where the defense possesses the ball. It’s the only game where a player is not allowed to reenter the game once he’s removed. Baseball is an eccentric sport and no obstacle is big enough for a comeback in baseball
