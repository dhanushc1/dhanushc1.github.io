CURRENT VOLLEYBALL

Volleyball is a very diverse sport with multiple different ways to play the game but the most popular choice of gameplay is Volleyball and Beach Volleyball. However, in the Olympics, Volleyball and Beach Volleyball are the only variations qualified. Volleyball made its Olympic appearance at the 1964 Tokyo Games and Beach Volleyball made its Olympic appearance in Atlanta in 1996. The International Olympic Committee (IOC) adopted Volleyball as a non-Olympic sport in 1949. Eight years later, on September 24, 1957, the IOC recognised Volleyball as an Olympic sport. In 1961, Volleyball was added as a medal sport for men and women at the Tokyo Games in 1964. The game of Volleyball never had a dominant team winning all of the games. It always varied from year to year. For example, the Japanese and the Soviet Union women’s teams dominated from 1964-1984, but then the balance of power has shifted to Cuba, then to China and then to Brazil. The United States men’s teams were prominent in the 1980s, Italy in the 1990s and Brazil in the 2000s.

Volleyball

Volleyball is a great sport that can be enjoyed by people of all ages and skill levels. It can be played year round as it can be played both indoors and outdoors. Aside from the pure enjoyment of the game, volleyball has many benefits.Each side includes a setter, one who keeps the in play for the others to score, two outside hitters, both are the lead hitter and their main objective is to score as many points as possible, an opposite hitter, one who has the job of receiving and attacking the ball in order to score and keep the ball in play, a middle blocker, one who is first line of defense against the opposing team’s hits and scores points for the team, and finally, the libero, one who is the main defender trying to keep the ball from touching the ground.
