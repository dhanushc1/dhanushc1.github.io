A game of cold and ice and fierce brutality. It’s not easy playing hockey. Moving at very fast speeds on slippery ice to being slammed by huge people. The biggest arena for hockey is called the NHL, with millions of fans, NHL games are fast and entertaining. In 2022, the Stanley Cup is between the Tampa Bay Lightning and the Colorado Avalanche. The game started off with the Avalanche getting a lead with them winning 4-3. The next game the Avalanche won by a fierce landslide with victory of 7-0.

Hockey

Hockey is fast paced and exciting with end to end action. There isn’t the delay between plays like other popular sports. The ice also adds an element of speed unlike any other. Shooting, passing, breakaways, one timer’s, big save. It will also build confidence and self esteem. It will teach your youngster discipline and teamwork, and it’s definitely good for their health.
