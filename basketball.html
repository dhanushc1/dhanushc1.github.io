CURRENT BASKETBALL

NBA season is very fast paced with games almost every day excluding the summer which is the off season. In 2022, all the teams played exceptionally and had some fascinating game play and will go down as a year to remember. The two teams who topped the others were the Boston Celtics and the Golden State Warriors. The Boston Celtics, who were the obvious underdogs, quickly surprised everyone and won against the Warriors by 12 points in their first game, but the Warriors came back swiftly and mopped the floor with the Celtics, with an astonishing 19 point victory. The cycle went on for the next two games with the Celtics winning game three, and Warriors winning game 4. It was in game 5 where the Warriors turned things around and beat the Celtics by ten points. Basketball

Basketball is one the most popular sports in America. Basketball was invented in 1891 by James Naismith. It grew popular in the 20th century and had its wide stretched claws all around the world. The NBA was established on November 1st 1946 and has grown to a multi-billion dollar empire enjoyed by fans all over the world.
