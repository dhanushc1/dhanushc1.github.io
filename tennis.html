CURRENT TENNIS

There are 4 major tournaments in tennis, the Wimbledon, the U.S open, the French open, and the Australian open. Each Grand Slam is played yearly and is a very high achievement to win. In 2022, Rafeal Nadal took home the Australian Slam for Men’s Singles, for Women’s Singles, Ashleigh Barty took home the title. In the French open, Rafeal Nadal won yet another title, another win to add to his impressive collection. In women’s singles, Iga Swiatek won the title, she is ranked number 1 in women’s tennis and is only 21 years old. Wimbledon is set to start on June 27th and the U.S open is set to start on August 29th . Tennis is a very fast paced sport, likewise so is the ranking which can be twisted and turned in a matter of one match.
