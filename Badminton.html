CURRENT BADMINTON

Badminton is a very fast paced game and similarly the schedule for it is very fast paced with tournaments lasting only a couple of days. You can check the schedule at https://olympics.com/en/news/badminton-2022-full-schedule. The sport is really quick with rally speeds up to 265 miles per hour. The sport has many fans with a total of 220 million people. Of those 220 million a majority is from Asia. The World Championship is scheduled to happen from August 21 to August 28. Some of the best players in Badminton, like Viktor Axelsen, Kento Momota, Anders Antonsen, and Chou Tien Chen will be playing.
